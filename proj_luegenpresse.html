Lügenpresse is a 5-computer-3-boys-2-AIs-1-girl impro band. We play on a bunch of self-made intelligent instruments (and regular ones). Lügenpresse is an homage to "Dunkeldeutschland" (the dark side of Germany), its enablers and their nonsense.



Human collaborators: olf, johannes, max

We are looking forward to booking requests.
