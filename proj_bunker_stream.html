Bunker streaming

Amsterdam, 2020/21
Take me back

Especially during the 2019/20/21 COVID pandemic, it can be hard for independent artists to present their work. The Vondelbunker is an autonomous cultural center in Amsterdam committed to supporting these artis since its opening in 2011. Live streaming can be a great tool to keep the DIY/DOT culture in Amsterdam and beyond alive. We ensembled a set up to stream and enhance artist performances by adding some visuals. Check out the results and the great people performing 👉

This is a joint venture with artists, and many other Vondelbunker volonteers..
